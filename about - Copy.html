Hockey!

Hockey is a sport where you have to get a small rubber puck into the goal on the other side of the ice with your team.

There are 6 player on the ice for each team and 20 players on a team. There is the Offense (3), Defense (2), and Goaltender (1).

There are 3 20 minute periods throughout the game with intermission after each period.

The goal of the sport is to score more goals than the other team before the time is up.
